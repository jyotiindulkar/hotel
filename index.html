1 Simple Booking

Booking your hotel should be a simple process, be it over the phone, via an agent, or online. If you have any questions during booking, there should be a knowledgeable, helpful person to guide you through. The hotel management should also be co-operative in terms of changes to your initial booking, or special requests.

2 Friendly First Contact

The first impression can be the last impression when it comes to staying at a hotel. The hospitality they offer needs to be neat and proper. The moment you first pull up to a hotel entrance or walk into the lobby, the initial interaction with the hosts is enough to make you aware of the type of place you’re staying.

3 Excellent Food and Drink

Even if you are planning on having most of your meals outside the hotel, a wide array of cuisines of excellent quality is always a good indicator of a hotel’s overall quality. Some of the guests are fascinated by their hotel’s fine dining, casual or pub-style dining, premium coffee shops as they travel to Phuket. This is what you need to look for while searching for your comfortable accommodation.

4 Cheerful Staff

You can easily understand the difference between fake cheerfulness and genuine warmth. Usually, the professionals working at great hotels are observant, empathetic, kind, and funny. Making the guests feel comfortable and important is what they love to do. Hence, you mustn’t overlook this special feature as well.

5 Amenities and Many more.

A fundamental difference between better hotels and ordinary ones is the amenities they provide. Firstly, they should give you a well-appointed guest room with every facility you’ve asked for. It includes high-quality bath and beauty products, beddings, towels, drawer and closet space, puffy hangers, bottled water, robes and slippers, wifi, snacks, beverages, and other goodies.
